About Us


Our Future

The goals for this institution are not only limited to the present. At the current moment,
we are working towards connecting with bigger art companies like @Sparketh or
@theartofeducation. What we truly wish for this platform is to be able to spread the love
of art to younger audiences that didn't get the chance. Our future will have more
engaging features, more artwork, and more connections to users.


Culture

The culture of art extends much farther from a drawing with pen and paper.
It's culture is one that has changed the earth from which we live on. Starting off with the art on cavern walls, to statues, to architectural designs, to what we now call life. Every aspect of our lives can be constituted as art, and with that, every aspect of art can be clasified as the culture that builds our society. Art is a way to express oneself, connect with others, and evolve.

Our Goal

Post Pandemic, people lost their jobs, lost touch with loved ones, and missed
out on life’s greatest experiences. However, art is a timeless way in which we can
express ourselves despite the circumstances and step into an immersive experience
filled with wonder. VirtuGal's mission is to cultivate a digital space for people
to connect, share, and explore artistic creations.


Your Benefits

By being a part of the VirtuGal community, you are able to get inspired by others works,
inspire others from your works, and have a fun time enjoying art and all of the intricate designs that artists across the globe have created. It also allow for exposure to certain companies that could be looking for workers. VirtuGal is a place that can benefit anyone, anywhere.

Our Members

We are all friends that have various interests that range from sports to music to
programming to art. However, we bonded in the club “TSA” at our high school and
enjoy competing in STEM focused events together. While we are only freshman in high
school, we hope to continue creating projects like this gallery
or solve a problem that can leave a lasting digital mark on society.

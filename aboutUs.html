MEET THE TEAM


Our Reasons

VirtuGal was created on the foundation that people from across the world can come together and view all kinds of artworks. After the Covid-19 lockdown, people had started to stop all of their traditions of embracing art. Because of that reason we say "Defeat Covid" and experience art like the past.






Our group Members

First, we have Santiago Espinoza. our creator. He writes the code and make the pages that our viewers love. Next, we have Hannah Parra and Trinity Nguyen, they specialize on reviewing and looking through art submissions. They make sure the best art gets displayed as appropriate for viewers. Next, we have Vaibav Submaranian and Khoa Le. They work on making sure that website is funtioning well and take care of sever-side issues. Last, but not least, we have Brian Chen, that works alongside Santiago Espinoza creating a website that looks and works perfectly. Focusing mainly on the CSS side of things, every design and image was managed by him.



Our Future

The goals for this institution are not only limited to the present. At the current moment, we are working towards connecting with bigger art companies like @Sparketh or @theartofeducation. What we truly wish for this platform, is to be able to spread the love of art to younger audiences that didn'd get the chance. Our future will have more engaging features, more artwork, and more connections to users.

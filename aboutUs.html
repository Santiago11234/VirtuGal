ABOUT US

OUR MISSION

During the pandemic, quarantine separated us from loved ones and prevented us from expressing ourselves. Art is a timeless way in which we can express ourselves and step into an immersive experience filled with wonder.

VirtuGal's mission is to cultivate an environment for people to connect, share, and explore artistic creations

AUCTIONS

VirtuGal auctions let users buy and display art while simultaneously raising money for the Bill and Melinda Gates foundation. Auctions take place from 8:00 AM to 10:00 PM and last around 1 hour each, although you can leave at any time.

Here at VirtuGal, we aim to make the world outside us better as well.

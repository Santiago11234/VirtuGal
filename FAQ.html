Frequently Asked Questions

How can I get involved?

Submitting art, viewing pieces, and leaving comments are all ways to support VirtuGal. We strive to build a community with a focus on artistic freedom and meaningful digital connections that offer a positive way to interact in the modern world.

Why visit virtual galleries?

Virtual galleries offer a way to view art straight from the comfort of your own home. It is also convenient and allows everyone free access to experience the beauty of art that shouldnâ€™t be a luxury few people are able to enjoy.

How many pieces can I submit?

You can submit as many pieces as you wish! Our team will filter through all pieces, and decide what to display in the gallery. Pieces generally take time to process and we try our best to cycle through and add new pieces every month. If you have any questions about your submission, feel free to email us.

Who are the featured artists?

Anyone! We have many art students submit pieces from their own portfolios. However, we welcome all submissions as long as they are appropriate for any age, as our gallery encourages diverse artistic expressions that represent all kinds of people.

FAQs

FAQ

Find the answers for the most frequently asked questions below

What is VirtuGal?

VirtuGal is a virtual art gallery that showcases submitted art and talent across the globe allowing individuals to express their creativity digitally reaching a wider audience. We also have exhibits and auctions in 1311 E 7th St, Fort Worth TX where you can visit artwork/hear guest

How do you submit art?

Submitting art is simple. All you have to do is submit the form to our gallery under the “Submit Art” tab and enter all the required information along with your art piece. You’re able to submit anytime, anywhere!

How many pieces can I submit?

You can submit as many pieces as you wish! Our team will filter through all pieces, and we will together decide what to display in the gallery. Pieces generally take time to process and we try our best to cycle through and add new pieces as every month. If you have any questions about your submission, feel free to message us under the “Contact Us” tab.

Who are the featured artists?

Anyone! We have many art students submit pieces from their own portfolios. However, we welcome all submissions as long as appropriate as our gallery encourages diverse artistic expressions that represent all kinds of people.

When and where will auctions and exhibits take place?
The location, dates, and details of our auctions and exhibits can be found on our “Events” page. Remember to sign up for our newsletter to receive special offers and tickets to see featured artists speak and showcase their artwork. We offer tours with every exhibit in order to provide information about the history of art styles, pieces, and a way to interact with others that share a love for art.
Who do our auctions and exhibits support?

When you attend an auction or exhibit you're supporting up and coming artists by getting their work into the world and providing them an audience to view their creations. We are a non profit organization, so all the proceeds go to fund art programs and spread awareness for art education.

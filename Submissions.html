RULES AND INFORMATION

Submit artwork as a pdf here. Please ensure it is appropriate for kids of all ages. Nudity, gore, or foul language of any kind will not be tolerated and will result in a ban on your account. When submitting, select your pdf, give it a title, and give it a description. We will not show your name on the website to avoid any segregation or bullying. Afterward, you're more than welcome to view your piece in our gallery and comment on it. We thank you for submitting your artwork, and we hope you enjoy seeing it!

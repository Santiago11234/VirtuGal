VirtuGal

Virtugal is a virtual art gallery that allows for people to submit, view, and comment on art. In a condensed website, you are able to experience the same feelings as being in a real art gallery. Covid-19 destroyed most peoples plans for art galleries, but with VirtuGal there is no excuse. The art can be viewed anywhere, at any time, and is constantly changing. The website is mainly user-input run and was created on the foundation that it would be the people that choose what they want to view. VirtuGal is a fun place for anyone to experience the immersive culture that is art.
